Pivoting from applied math into software development, I am an engineer that stacks statistical analysis and mathematical knowledge on top of practical programming techniques to build quality, scalable applications.

Driven by intersecting my analytical background and minute attention to detail with the creative outlet of programming, my goal is to simplify complex concepts into programs that people can use in their everyday. With my experience in front-end design and back-end infrastructure, I construct efficient and elegant programming solutions.
